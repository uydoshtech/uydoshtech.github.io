Delete Your UyDosh Account

You can request to delete your UyDosh account and all associated personal data. Please follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Log in to your UyDosh account via the app or website.
  2. Go to Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Click the Delete My Account button and confirm your request.
  4. Your account will be scheduled for deletion.

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Name, email, phone number, and profile photo.
  • All your listings and posts.
  • Messages and chat history.

Data That May Be Retained:

Anonymous aggregated data (e.g., usage statistics) may be kept for analytics and improving the service.

Deletion Timeline: All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

If you encounter any issues or need help deleting your account, please contact our support team.
